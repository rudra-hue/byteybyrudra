My approach to learning DSA has been methodical:

  2. Moving to more complex structures (Stacks, Queues, Trees, Graphs)
  3. Learning essential algorithms (Searching, Sorting, Recursion)
  4. Practicing problem-solving on platforms like LeetCode and HackerRank
  5. Applying DSA knowledge in real projects and coding contests
